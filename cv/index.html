Academic Interests

  • Pharmacometrics
    • PK-PD (pharmacokinetics and pharmacodynamics) and Population Modeling: Optimizes drug design, predicts efficacy and safety, and addresses target-mediated drug disposition. Population PK/PD helps assess patient variability and supports cross-species scaling for clinical trials and precision medicine.
    • QSP (Quantitative Systems Pharmacology): Integrates mechanistic models with PK/PD data to guide drug development, optimize dosing, and enhance understanding of complex biological systems.
    • Immunogenicity: Addresses immune responses and nonlinear kinetics in large molecule drugs, ensuring therapeutic outcomes in clinical and industrial applications.
    • Regulatory and Clinical Exemptions: Supports regulatory submissions and potentially streamlines clinical trials with trial exemptions, accelerating drug development and approval.
    • Web-based Models: Used for simulating drug behavior, visualizing results, and facilitating real-time decision-making in collaboration with clinicians and regulators.
  • Pharmacology and Pathology
    • Drug Transport and BBB Interaction: Investigated transporters' roles (e.g., OCTs, MATE1) in drug disposition across the blood-brain barrier, focusing on how peripheral diseases like liver pathology affect CNS drug exposure.
    • CNS Pharmacokinetics and Drug Metabolism: Explored CNS drug distribution and efficacy in the context of metabolic changes in peripheral organs.
    • Peripheral Organ Pathology and CNS Dysfunction: Examined the impact of peripheral diseases, like liver injury, on CNS drug metabolism using in vivo and in vitro models.
    • Neuroprotective Drug Development: Researched the development of drugs aimed at protecting CNS function, particularly in cases where peripheral pathology leads to secondary CNS damage.
    • Cancer Therapy with Large Molecule Drugs: Focused on developing and optimizing Antibody-Drug Conjugates (ADCs) for the treatment of cancers such as breast and gastric cancer, with an emphasis on PK-PD and clinical outcomes.
